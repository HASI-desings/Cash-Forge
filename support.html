Frequently Asked Questions

How do I deposit funds?

Go to the Home page and click "Deposit". Send USDT (TRC20) to the displayed address and upload your transaction screenshot. Funds will appear after admin approval.

When can I withdraw?

Withdrawals adhere to a specific schedule based on your VIP level. Generally, withdrawals are processed within 24 hours. Check the "Wallet" section for your specific limits.

How does the team bonus work?

You earn commissions from 3 levels of referrals (Level 1, 2, and 3). Share your invite link found in the "Team" or "Profile" tab to start building your network.

I forgot my PIN code.

For security reasons, PIN resets must be handled manually. Please contact our Telegram support with your registered email and ID for assistance.
