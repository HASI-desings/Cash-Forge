Frequently Asked Questions

How do I deposit funds?

Go to the Deposit page. Enter the PKR amount you wish to pay, and the system will calculate the equivalent USDT. Send USDT (TRC20 or BEP20) to the displayed address and upload your transaction screenshot. Funds will appear after admin approval.

When can I withdraw?

Withdrawals are limited to once every 7 days and adhere to a strict schedule based on your active package. Withdrawals are closed every Sunday. You must also have a configured USDT wallet in the Settings section.

How does the team bonus work?

You earn commissions from 3 levels of referrals (Level A: 5%, Level B: 2%, Level C: 1%) when they complete daily tasks. You also receive a one-time bonus when a downline member purchases an investment package.

I forgot my PIN code.

For security reasons, transaction PIN resets must be handled manually by the administration. Please contact our Telegram support with your registered email and ID for assistance.
