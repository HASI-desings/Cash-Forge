Common Questions

How do I recharge my wallet?
Go to the Dashboard and click on "Deposit". Select your amount, copy the TRC20 address, and send the exact amount. Upload the screenshot for verification.
When are withdrawals processed?
Withdrawals are processed Monday to Friday, from 10:00 AM to 6:00 PM. Weekend withdrawals are disabled. Processing time is usually 30 minutes to 24 hours.
How does the Team Commission work?
You earn 25% from direct referrals (Level 1), 10% from Level 2, and 5% from Level 3. Commissions are credited instantly to your wallet.
What is the minimum withdrawal?
The minimum withdrawal amount is 1,000 PKR. A standard service fee of 7% applies to all withdrawals.
